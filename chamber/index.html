Project Overview

The Purpose of the Chamber of Commerce website is to create an online platform that connects local businesses, promotes economic growth, and provides valuable resources for entrepreneurs. This website will offer a variety of features, including a business directory, event calendar, membership portal, and community news.

Design & Aesthetics

The design of the website will focus on professionalism, trust, and accessibility. The following key design elements will be incorporated:

  • Color Schema: The primary colors will be shades of blue (for trust and professionalism) and grey tones (for elegance and neutrality).
  • Typography: The primary font will be 'Arial' for a clean, modern look, while 'Georgia' will be used for headings to add elegance and a traditional feel.
  • Responsive Design: The website will be fully responsive to provide an optimal viewing experience on all devices (desktop, tablet, and mobile).

Website Features

The Chamber of Commerce website will include the following main features:

  • Business Directory - A searchable directory of local businesses by category, location, and other filters.
  • Event Calendar - A calendar to display upcoming events, workshops, and networking opportunities.
  • Membership Portal - A secure login area for members to access exclusive content and services.
  • News & Updates - A section for the latest news on local business trends, chamber activities, and community events.
  • Contact Information - A page with contact details, office hours, and a contact form for inquiries.

Next Steps

We are excited to move forward with this project. The next steps include gathering content, defining the site architecture, and beginning the development process.
